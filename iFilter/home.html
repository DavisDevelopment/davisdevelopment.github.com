Download iFilter.js

No Flash!

iFilter.js uses nothing but JavaScript to perform it's effects, so no need to deal with other technologies your users may or may not have.

Display Any Photo!

iFilter can allow embedding of images in RAW format, SVG format, and PSD's! So no photo gets left behind.

Lightning Fast!

iFilter optionally uses Parallel.js to vastly increase it's speed, so you and your users don't have to wait. After all, why should you?

 Welcome to the iFilter page! Here, you can learn all about iFilter.js, and the progress I'm making with it. Currently, effects like Grayscale, saturate, desaturate, sepia, and pixelate. It's completely pluggable, so you can add your own effects if you like. Feel free to check out the demo, and comment and tell me what you think. I love to get feedback.
