iFilter.js

Download iFilter.js

No Flash!

iFilter.js uses nothing but JavaScript to perform it's effects, so no need to deal with other technologies your users may or may not have.

Display Any Photo!

iFilter can allow embedding of images in RAW format, SVG format, and PSD's! So no photo gets left behind.

Lightning Fast!

iFilter optionally uses Parallel.js to vastly increase it's speed, so you and your users don't have to wait. After all, why should you?

The spec is coming very soon, and will include all the currently supported methods by iFilter. Until then, please be patient. I'm working on it in every spare second I have.
